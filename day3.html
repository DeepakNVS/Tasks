Chicken Tikka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken breasts or thighs, skin removed
  • 2 tbsp tandoori masala powder
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp garlic and ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the tandoori masala powder, yogurt, lemon juice, garlic and ginger paste, vegetable oil, and salt.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken and mix well to ensure all the pieces are coated.
  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  5. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  6. Remove the chicken from the marinade and place on the baking tray in a single layer.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and starting to char.
  8. Serve hot with naan bread, rice, or salad.
